Baccarat Rules (Simplified):
  • Bet size: $100 per hand.
  • Player and Banker each start with two cards.
  • Cards 2–9 = face value; 10/J/Q/K = 0; A = 1.
  • Totals are modulo 10 (e.g., 15 → 5).
  • Higher total wins; equal totals = Tie.
  • Player/Banker bets pay 2x; Tie pays 8x.
