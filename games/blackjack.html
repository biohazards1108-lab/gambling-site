Blackjack Rules (Simplified Casino Style):
  • Bet size: $100 per hand (fixed in this demo).
  • Player and dealer each receive two cards at the start.
  • Number cards count as their value; face cards count as 10; Aces count as 11 or 1.
  • Player may Hit (take another card) or Stand (keep current total).
  • Dealer must Hit until reaching at least 17.
  • If player exceeds 21, player busts and loses the bet.
  • If dealer exceeds 21, dealer busts and player wins 2x the bet.
  • If neither busts, higher total wins; equal totals result in a push (bet returned).
